Hi!

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Economics at the Orfalea College of Business at Cal Poly. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the Johns Hopkins University.

My research interests are in microeconomic and statistical theory with a focus on model misspecification, social learning, and decision theory.

You can reach me at my email: aghosh10[at]calpoly[dot]edu.
